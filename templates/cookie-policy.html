Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how MZTVENTURES uses them on our website, and how you can manage your cookie preferences.

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how MZTVENTURES uses them on our website, and how you can manage your cookie preferences. Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit and interact with our website.

By using the MZTVENTURES website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. If you do not agree with our cookie practices, you can adjust your browser settings or opt out as described below.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website. They contain information that can be sent back to the website on subsequent visits, allowing the website to recognize you and remember certain information about your preferences and activities.

Cookies serve various purposes including:

  • Remembering your login information and preferences
  • Tracking your browsing behavior and interactions
  • Personalizing your user experience
  • Analyzing website traffic and usage patterns
  • Displaying targeted advertisements

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Session Cookies

Session cookies are temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser. They are used to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember items in your shopping cart
  • Track your navigation within the website
  • Enable basic website functionality
3.2 Persistent Cookies

Persistent cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you manually delete them. They are used to:

  • Remember your login credentials
  • Store your language preferences
  • Track your browsing history across visits
  • Remember your booking history and preferences
3.3 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as:

  • User authentication and account access
  • Security and fraud detection
  • Content delivery and page loading
  • Shop cart and checkout processes
  • Cookie preference management
3.4 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website. They collect information about:

  • Number of visitors and repeat visitors
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referral sources and exit pages
  • Device type and operating system
  • User interactions and behavior patterns

Note: We use Google Analytics for tracking website usage. Google may use this data for their own purposes. For more information, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

3.5 Marketing and Advertising Cookies

These cookies track your browsing behavior across websites to:

  • Display personalized advertisements
  • Measure advertising campaign effectiveness
  • Create user interest profiles
  • Retarget users with relevant travel offers
  • Track conversion rates from advertisements
3.6 Third-Party Cookies

Third-party cookies are set by service providers other than MZTVENTURES, including:

  • Social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter)
  • Payment processors and financial service providers
  • Analytics providers (Google Analytics, Hotjar)
  • Advertising networks and ad exchanges
  • Customer service and chat providers

5. Why We Use Cookies

MZTVENTURES uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Functionality: Enabling core website features, user authentication, and personalization
  • Performance: Optimizing website speed, reliability, and user experience
  • Analytics: Understanding visitor behavior to improve our services
  • Marketing: Delivering relevant travel offers and advertisements
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraudulent activities
  • Compliance: Meeting legal and regulatory requirements

6. How Long Do Cookies Last?

The duration of cookies varies depending on their type and purpose:

  • Session Cookies: Automatically deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a specific period (ranging from days to years) or until manually deleted
  • Analytics Cookies: Typically stored for 2 years from the date of collection
  • Marketing Cookies: Generally stored for 3-12 months depending on the platform

7. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

7.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, a cookie consent banner will appear. You can:

  • Accept All: Accept all cookies including marketing and analytics
  • Reject Non-Essential: Accept only essential cookies
  • Customize: Choose which types of cookies to accept
7.2 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your web browser settings. Here's how for popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Clear browsing data
  • Internet Explorer: Tools > Internet Options > General > Browsing history Delete
7.3 Do Not Track (DNT) Signals

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" feature. If you enable DNT, MZTVENTURES will respect your preference and limit the use of tracking cookies. However, please note that not all third-party services respond to DNT signals.

7.4 Third-Party Opt-Out Options

You can opt out of specific third-party cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on
  • Facebook Ads: Visit your Facebook Ads Preferences
  • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI): NAI Opt-Out Tool
  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA): DAA Opt-Out Tool

8. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling cookies may impact your experience on our website. Specifically:

  • You may not be able to log in to your account
  • Your preferences and bookings may not be saved
  • Shopping cart functionality may not work properly
  • Personalized recommendations may not be available
  • Website performance may be affected

Important: Essential cookies are required for the website to function. We recommend keeping essential cookies enabled while allowing you to control non-essential cookies.

9. Privacy and Data Security

Cookies can store personal information, and we take data security seriously. We:

  • Use secure connection (HTTPS) for all cookie transmission
  • Encrypt sensitive data stored in cookies
  • Restrict access to cookie data to authorized personnel only
  • Comply with international data protection regulations
  • Do not sell cookie-based data to third parties

For more information about how we handle your personal data, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

10. International Compliance

Our cookie practices comply with international data protection regulations:

  • GDPR (EU): We obtain explicit consent before storing non-essential cookies
  • CCPA (California): We provide options to opt-out of cookie tracking and data selling
  • ePrivacy Directive: We inform users about cookies and obtain necessary consents
  • Global Standards: We follow best practices for cookie disclosure and management

11. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or technology. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. Your continued use of the website following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

We recommend reviewing this policy regularly to stay informed about how we use cookies.
